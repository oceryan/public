Biography

I am an Assistant Professor of Operations Management at the LeBow College of Business, Drexel University.

My research interests are primarily in dynamic pricing, revenue management, and supply chain management. My recent work has appeared in Manufacturing & Service Operations Management and Production & Operations Management. A list of my published and working papers are provided subsequently.

I teach courses on operations research, operations management, business statistics, inventory models, and revenue management, and was the recipient of Dean’s Excellence in Teaching Award (2016) at the LeBow College of Business.

I received a dual Ph.D degree in Industrial and Operations Engineering and in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Michigan. Prior to joining Drexel University, I was a lecturer and postdoctoral research fellow in the Department of Operations and Management Science at the Ross School of Business at the University of Michigan.
